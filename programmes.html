Our Programmes

Four pathways to mastering the unknown.

01. Theoretical Architecture

Bachelor of Science

Move beyond bricks and mortar. Learn to design non-Euclidean structures and impossible buildings that exist only in the mind—until you build them.

  • Focus: Structural Paradoxes
  • Duration: 4 Years

02. Quantum Literature

Bachelor of Arts

Study texts that change every time they are read. Analyze the intersection of narrative theory and particle physics in a non-linear timeline.

  • Focus: Variable Endings
  • Duration: 3 Years

03. Advanced Philanthropy

Master of Social Works

Don't just give back—strategize forward. Learn to solve global crises before they happen using predictive algorithms and ethical funding.

  • Focus: Pre-emptive Care
  • Duration: 2 Years

04. Astro-Linguistics

Bachelor of Science

The universe is speaking; learn how to listen. This program focuses on deciphering, translating, and replying to deep-space radio signals.

  • Focus: Alien Syntax
  • Duration: 4 Years
